Building a healthier and more sustainable future

Founded on the core values of solidarity , equity ,and shared responsability,the ASEN association is a non-profit and apolitical organization committed at the heart of socio-economic and health development in Africa. Facing the major health and environmental challenges of our time , we mobilize on a daily basis to provide concrete support to vulnerable populations while working to safeguard biodiversity. Driven by a purely social interest , we believe that a protected environment is the cornestone of a healthy population.

Our pillars of action

01. Health Impact

We work daily to improve healthcare access, run disease prevention campaigns, and provide emergency medical assistance to the most disadvantaged patients.

02. Ecological Commitment

Protecting our planet is inseparable from human health. ASEN deploys on-the-ground initiatives to preserve the environment and raise community awareness about eco-friendly practices.

03. Innovation and Capacity

We support capacity building for local healthcare facilities and encourage scientific research to sustain tailored and innovative health solutions.

About

Getting to know us means understanding our cause. Dive into our history, discover the core values that drive us, and explore the mission we have set for ourselves.

Our History

ASEN (Action for Health and the Environment) is a non-profit organization committed to improving health and environmental protection.
Operating primarily in Cameroon, it aims to address the health and environmental challenges faced by communities, particularly the most vulnerable.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote the well-being of populations through disease prevention, improved access to healthcare, and the sustainable protection of the environment.
ASEN works to foster balanced development where human health and the environment are closely intertwined.

Our aims

  • Improving healthcare access for vulnerable populations.
  • Promoting disease prevention through awareness campaigns.
  • Providing medical and humanitarian assistance during emergencies.
  • Strengthening the capacity of local healthcare facilities.
  • Encouraging health research and innovation to find sustainable solutions.
  • Promoting environmental protection.
  • Integrating health and environment into public policies.
  • Developing strategic partnerships to maximize the impact of our actions.

Our activities

ASEN conducts various activities to achieve its goals, including:

  • Organizing awareness campaigns on health and environmental issues.
  • Providing medical care and assistance to vulnerable populations.
  • Collaborating with local and international partners to strengthen the capacity of healthcare facilities.
  • Promoting health research and innovation.

Our values

  • Engagement :We are committed to making a positive impact on the communities we serve.
  • Collaboration :We believe in teamwork and collaboration to reach our goals.
  • Transparency : We pledge to be open and honest in all our actions and communication.
  • Sustainability :We aim to advance sustainable solutions benefiting both human health and the environment.

our vision statement

Building a society where health and the environment are priorities, and where every individual has access to quality healthcare and a healthy, sustainable, and balanced environment, thereby contributing to a better future for all.

Our Projects

ASEN leads various projects to improve health and protect the environment. Here are some of our flagship projects:

  • Health Awareness Campaign : We conduct community-based awareness campaigns aimed at promoting positive health outcomes and disease prevention.
  • Medical Aid Project :Our organization provides essential medical assistance to vulnerable groups, notably in response to public health emergencies.
  • Institutional Strengthening Project :We work in partnership with local health structures to enhance their capacities and optimize the quality of healthcare delivery.
  • Health Research Project :"We encourage research and innovation in health to find sustainable solutions to healthcare challenges.
  • Environmental Conservation Project : We lead initiatives to raise awareness about environmental protection and promote sustainable practices.
